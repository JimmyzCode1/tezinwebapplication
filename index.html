How VR Lending Works

Follow these steps to secure your hardware:

  • 1
    Reserve Online

    Select your headset model and booking dates through this portal.

  • 3
    Same-Day Return

    Headsets must be returned the same day by 17:00. Schedule a lab appointment to request multi-day extensions.
